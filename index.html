Our Products

We develop a range of AI-powered assistive hardware products able to translate between sign languages and spoken languages, supporting multiple sign and spoken languages, and targeting diverse sectors, including public institutions, educational environments, corporate settings, and government agencies. All our devices include a microphone, speaker, and camera to support the bidirectional communication, and can also be connected to external input/output devices. We also provide customizable hardware formats to fit different environments and user needs. For now, our primary advertising and outreach efforts focus on three flagship products:

Product

SignAvatar Station Pro

The SignAvatar Station Pro device features a full-body digital avatar on a large vertical screen.

Product

SignAvatar Station Lite

The SignAvatar Station Lite device is compact and tablet-based. It is optimized for smaller spaces and environments that require a portable and flexible solution.

Product

SignPodium

SignPodium is a podium with a vertical display screen integrated into the stand (facing the audience), while the camera and microphone are positioned on the speaker's side to capture input.

About Us

We are ChatSign Technology, a startup company focused on providing assistive technology for the deaf and hard-of-hearing (DHH) community. Our products are AI-chip-powered devices that translate speech into sign language and sign language into speech through a digital avatar. Our company is a spin-off from New York University Abu Dhabi (NYUAD) and our AI models are developed in collaboration with the Embodied AI and Robotics Lab at the university, with an exclusive license permitting commercialization filed.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To develop advanced AI chips and integrate them into accessible hardwares that enhance accessibility for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community while providing scalable and commercially viable solutions for businesses and institutions.

Vision

To become a global leader in assistive technology by integrating advanced Application specific AI chip (ASAIC) solutions into public and professional environments, promoting inclusivity while driving sustainable business growth and technological innovation.
