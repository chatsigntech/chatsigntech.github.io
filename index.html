About Us

Our Company

ChatSign is revolutionizing communication for the deaf and hard-of-hearing (DHH) communities by providing real-time sign language translation tools, allowing DHH individuals to talk in their primary language, and be included in public spaces. Our innovative solutions, SignAvatar and SignGlasses, and possible future products, bridge the gap between sign and spoken languages, empowering users to attend live events, engage with media, and complete everyday tasks effortlessly. Leveraging advanced AI, we are committed to fostering independence, inclusivity, and equality, enabling seamless interactions and creating a more connected world for everyone.


Join us in breaking down communication barriers.
