At ChatSign Technology, our mission is simple: bridge the communication gap between the Deaf/Hard-of-Hearing and the hearing world through AI. We're a humble, passionate team building tech that serves people—and if that speaks to you, we'd love to have you with us.

Our Values

Empathy First
We build with empathy, guided by voices often unheard.
Mission Driven
We're driven by purpose, not routine—what we build matters.
Initiative Over Instructions
We value independent thinking, and creative action—leadership here means ownership, not hierarchy.
Confidently Humble
We stay grounded, embrace feedback, and collaborate—because confidence grows strongest with humility.
Build for Impact
We measure success by impact, not credit—what matters is creating lasting, meaningful change.

Learning, Together

We're all growing—professionally and personally. Mistakes are welcomed as part of progress but we don't tolerate:

  • Elitism, ego and exclusion
  • Performative inclusion without action
  • Disregard for accessibility or the people we serve
